About the project

ExoHunter is a web application that allows users to explore and analyze light curve data from NASA's Kepler, K2, and TESS missions. The app leverages machine learning algorithms to identify potential exoplanet candidates, making it easier for both amateur and professional astronomers to contribute to the search for worlds beyond our solar system.

ExoHunter Hunting for Exoplanets with AI

A next-generation project that combines open NASA data, deep learning and curiosity to discover new worlds beyond our solar system.

public Challenge

Developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge: “A World Away – Hunting for Exoplanets with AI”.

ExoHunter uses datasets from Kepler, K2 and TESS missions to train machine learning models capable of identifying planetary candidates based on the transit method — analyzing variations in stellar light.
